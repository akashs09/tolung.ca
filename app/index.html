Recently relocated to our new location in central Toronto at 1849 Yonge Street we continue to look forward in collaborating with you to respiratory care.

GOAL: OUTSTANDING RESPIRATORY CARE

Our clinic philosophy is patient focused care where where our smaller environment aims to foster a close-knit relationship with our patients and medical care partners.

Our Pulmonary Function Laboratory offer a broad array of medical tests imperative to your diagnosis.

Some of these include:

  • Full PFT's
  • Methacholine Challenge Test (asthma)
  • Arterial/Venous/Capillary Blood Gases analysis

Please Visit our Labs page for more information.

Our Respirologists are well experienced in many respiratory disorders. We are pleased to offer speciality programs at our clinic:

  • Comprehensive asthma care including asthma education, pediatric asthma and Xolair therapy
  • Comprehensive COPD assessment and prescription for rehabilitation programs
  • Cough Clinic - Assessment and Management of chronic cough
  • Better Breathing Clinic Assessment and management of dyspnea

Please visit our Patients page for more information.
